Privacy Policy

Better Morning

Last updated: March 2025

Better Morning respects your privacy. This app is designed to work entirely on your device and does not collect personal data.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Specifically:

No accounts or user profiles

No names, emails, or identifiers

No analytics or tracking data

No cloud storage or servers

No third-party SDKs for tracking or advertising

All routines, tasks, and progress data are stored locally on your device only.

App Data

Better Morning stores the following data locally on your device:

Morning routines (celebrity or custom)

Task completion status

Daily and weekly progress summaries

App settings (such as notification preferences)

This data never leaves your device unless you delete the app or reset your device.

Notifications

Better Morning may send local notifications to remind you of your morning routine.
These notifications are scheduled and handled entirely on your device.

We do not receive notification data and cannot see when or whether notifications are delivered.

In-App Purchases

Better Morning offers a one-time in-app purchase to unlock custom routine creation.

Purchases are processed securely by Apple via the App Store.
We do not receive or store any payment information.

Third-Party Services

Better Morning does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time by:

Using the Reset all data option in the app’s settings, or

Deleting the app from your device

Children’s Privacy

Better Morning does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.
Because the app does not collect personal data at all, no special data handling is required.
